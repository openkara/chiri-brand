# Chiri Positioning Notes
**Consolidated from customer feedback sessions, May 2026**
**No em-dashes. No "AI Sherpa." No employee ranges in public-facing content.**

---

## Core Message

**Chiri helps mid-market companies adopt AI safely and actually operationalize it.**

This is the single sentence that everything else should support. It answers three questions at once: who (mid-market), what (adopt AI), and how (safely and operationally, not just theoretically).

---

## The Mid-Market Thesis

We specialize in operationally complex small-to-medium companies. We believe these companies are positioned to grow faster in the AI era, not slower. They don't have big company problems. They move faster, decide faster, and adapt faster. Chiri helps them build the AI infrastructure to accelerate that advantage and stay ahead of the curve.

This is a positive thesis, not a consolation prize. The mid-market is not "too small for Palantir." It is the fastest-moving segment in the market, and Chiri is purpose-built for it.

---

## The Five Supporting Messages

### 1. Governed by Design
Chiri gives companies auditability, access controls, and structure that reduce fear around rogue agents. Every agent action is logged. Every decision is traceable. Hierarchical data access controls ensure the right people see the right data. This is the message that lands hardest with CIOs, CISOs, and anyone who has been burned by shadow AI.

**What resonated in feedback:** "Governance + safety for enterprise AI" was the strongest reaction. The idea that Chiri helps prevent rogue agents and adds full auditability felt like a real market gap.

---

### 2. Learns How Work Gets Done
Instead of forcing teams to document everything upfront, Chiri captures workflows, builds context over time, and grows with the company. The platform uses screen recording and workflow mapping to observe how people actually work, automatically annotates tasks and clicks, and identifies automation opportunities without requiring manual process documentation.

**What resonated in feedback:** "Self-learning system" was called out as a major differentiator. The idea that the platform builds knowledge passively rather than requiring heavy manual setup. Explicitly noted as "easier to show than tell, but powerful once trusted."

---

### 3. Moves at the Speed of the Spoken Word
Chiri helps turn conversations, ideas, and live workflow knowledge into execution faster than traditional documentation-heavy systems. Voice-driven workflow creation. 30-minute implementation cycles from conversation to execution. No prompt engineering required.

**What resonated in feedback:** "Speed of the spoken word" was called out as a memorable positioning idea. Turning spoken brainstorming into execution quickly, without forcing people to translate everything into written process docs first.

---

### 4. Hides the AI Complexity
Users should not have to think about prompt engineering, ontology design, or system orchestration. Chiri abstracts that away. The value is that AI fades into the background while people just do their jobs. The word "ontology" should never appear in customer-facing materials unless the customer specifically asks about it.

**What resonated in feedback:** "Abstraction of AI complexity" was called out as important. Users should not need to think about prompt engineering or even "ontology." The value is that AI fades into the background.

---

### 5. Meet You Where You Are
Low-friction entry. No rip-and-replace. No new dashboards. No new logins. Chiri deploys inside the tools your team already uses. The system learns from how people already work and builds the ontology as it goes. This is the zero-disruption promise.

**What resonated in feedback:** "Low-friction adoption / meet you where you are" landed well. Users wouldn't need to fully document workflows up front.

---

## Three Punchy Positioning Statements

These are the three statements that emerged from the feedback session as the strongest distillations of the Chiri value proposition:

1. **Chiri helps mid-market companies move at the speed of the spoken word, turning operational knowledge into governed AI workflows.**

2. **Chiri is the AI operating system for companies that want automation without losing control.**

3. **Chiri helps teams adopt AI without the friction of documenting everything first, learning workflows as work happens.**

---

## Product Architecture (Internal Reference)

Three core components:

1. **Data integration layer** — connects all storage systems into a searchable format
2. **Ontology system** — living, growing knowledge base that matures with the company
3. **Workflow mapping** — screen recording tool that captures and annotates user tasks, identifies automation opportunities, and shows users where to click for specific tasks

Security and governance:
- Hierarchical data access controls
- Full auditability for human and agent actions
- Data segmentation by user roles
- SOC 2 compliance in progress

## Competitive Context

| Competitor | Who They Are | What They Miss |
|---|---|---|
| **Microsoft Copilot** | AI copilot inside Microsoft 365 | Requires Microsoft ecosystem, no cross-system orchestration |
| **Salesforce Agentforce** | CRM-native AI agents | Locked to Salesforce data, no cross-system governance |
| **UiPath** | RPA + LLM for existing automation | Strongest where RPA already exists, limited for greenfield |
| **Kamiwaza AI** | GenAI engine for private data across any hardware | Enterprise-focused, infrastructure-heavy, not people-first |
| **Palantir** | Enterprise AI operating system | Priced and scoped for Fortune 500, not mid-market |

**Chiri's gap:** The only platform that (1) sits above all tools simultaneously, (2) captures and replicates the tacit operational knowledge of human workers, (3) deploys governed agents inside the tools employees already use, and (4) is built by HR operators who understand the human side of change.
