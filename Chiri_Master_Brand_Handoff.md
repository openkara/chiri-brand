## 2. Brand Voice Guide

### The One-Sentence Brief
Chiri sounds like a really good-hearted, whip-smart but humble person who you want to work with and work for. They have been around the block, are not knee-jerked by random trends, really see humans and people, get people and processes, and have the wisdom and the calm to sit back and watch stuff happening and be really incisive and smart — not from an intellectual showing-off place, but from an "I can make this accessible because I know it" and "you're in good hands with me" place.

### A Note on the Name
When you say "Chiri" out loud, it sounds warm. Almost sweet. We don't want to be cute — but we do want to be approachable. The name signals something human and accessible. That is a feature, not a bug. Lean into it.

### The Seven Principles

**1. Curiosity First (Most Important)**
Every post, every email, every piece of content should open with a question. Not a statement. Not a declaration. A genuine question that opens up empathy and emotion before we go into our value proposition.

*Sounds like:* "What's the one workflow eating your team's week right now?"
*Does not sound like:* "AI is transforming the workforce. Here's how Chiri can help."

**2. Calm Authority, Not Performance**
Chiri has seen a lot. It does not need to shout. Steady, measured, confident — not because it is trying to project confidence, but because it has earned it.

**3. People-First, Always**
The voice always comes back to the person in the workflow. Not the system. Not the model. The person.

**4. Accessible Intelligence**
Chiri knows complex things and explains them simply. The voice never shows off. It makes things clear.

**5. Specific and Grounded**
The 1,500-person healthcare group. The HR director answering benefits questions at 9pm. Specificity is what separates Chiri from the AI commentariat.

**6. Contrarian Where It Counts, Never for Sport**
Chiri is willing to say the thing nobody else is saying. But only when it is true and useful.

**7. Warm Closer**
Every Chiri post ends with a sense of "you're in good hands." Not a hard sell. A genuine invitation.

### The LinkedIn Post Formula
1. Open with a question (under 210 characters)
2. Name the specific pain or insight (one concrete observation)
3. Tell a specific story or cite a specific source
4. Connect to Chiri's thesis — warm, grounded, never a pitch
5. Close with a question or soft invitation
6. 3-5 hashtags lower in the post

### What Chiri Says vs. Does Not Say

| Chiri Says | Chiri Does Not Say |
|---|---|
| People power business. | Our AI replaces your workforce. |
| The hardest part was never the software. | Our software solves everything. |
| You're in good hands. | Disrupt. Transform. Revolutionize. |
| Digital workers handle execution. Humans retain judgment. | AI is smarter than your team. |
| What's the one workflow eating your team's week? | Book a demo today! |
| Designed to disappear. | We'll be your long-term partner. |

## 3. Positioning Notes

### Core Message
**Chiri helps mid-market companies adopt AI safely and actually operationalize it.**

### The Mid-Market Thesis
We specialize in operationally complex small-to-medium companies. We believe these companies are positioned to grow faster in the AI era, not slower. They don't have big company problems. They move faster, decide faster, and adapt faster.

### Five Supporting Messages

1. **Governed by Design** — Auditability, access controls, and structure that reduce fear around rogue agents. Every agent action logged. Every decision traceable.

2. **Learns How Work Gets Done** — Instead of forcing teams to document everything upfront, Chiri captures workflows, builds context over time, and grows with the company.

3. **Moves at the Speed of the Spoken Word** — Turn conversations, ideas, and live workflow knowledge into execution. No prompt engineering required.

4. **Hides the AI Complexity** — Users should not have to think about prompt engineering, ontology design, or system orchestration. Chiri abstracts that away.

5. **Meet You Where You Are** — No rip-and-replace. No new dashboards. No new logins. Deploys inside the tools your team already uses.

### Three Punchy Positioning Statements
1. Chiri helps mid-market companies move at the speed of the spoken word, turning operational knowledge into governed AI workflows.
2. Chiri is the AI operating system for companies that want automation without losing control.
3. Chiri helps teams adopt AI without the friction of documenting everything first, learning workflows as work happens.

### Competitive Context

| Competitor | Who They Are | What They Miss |
|---|---|---|
| Microsoft Copilot | AI copilot inside Microsoft 365 | Requires Microsoft ecosystem, no cross-system orchestration |
| Salesforce Agentforce | CRM-native AI agents | Locked to Salesforce data, no cross-system governance |
| UiPath | RPA + LLM | Strongest where RPA already exists |
| Kamiwaza AI | GenAI engine for private data | Enterprise-focused, infrastructure-heavy, not people-first |
| Palantir | Enterprise AI OS | Priced for Fortune 500, not mid-market |

## 4. Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Background (light):** Warm Alabaster `#F2EDE6`
- **Background (dark):** Deep Warm Charcoal `#1C1A18`
- **Primary text:** Near-black `#1C1A18`
- **Accent:** Chiri Orange `#E85D22`
- **Muted text:** `#6B6560`
- **Border:** `#D2CDC6`

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Extra Bold (800 weight)
- **Body:** Montserrat Light (300 weight) or Inter
- **Labels:** Montserrat Bold (700 weight), all caps, letter-spaced

### Logo Rules
- On dark backgrounds: use the white/light version of the logo
- On light/cream backgrounds: use the dark version (Chiri wordmark in `#1C1A18`)
- The triangle icon always stays Chiri Orange

### Social Image Template
- 1080x1080px square
- Orange left-border rule (6px)
- Label in orange caps at top
- Large Montserrat Bold quote text
- Chiri logo (dark or light depending on background) bottom left
- No "Chiri Insights" subtitle
- No direct quotes in the image (use paraphrased versions)

## 13. Dream App Vision

The ideal content and marketing growth app for Chiri would do the following:

### Content Planning
- All posts visible in a calendar view with status: Draft / Scheduled / Published
- One-click copy of post text
- One-click download of associated image or video clip
- Tagging instructions visible per post
- Date picker for scheduling

### Publishing Integration
- Direct publish to LinkedIn company page via LinkedIn API
- Direct publish to X via X API
- Optional: Buffer, Hootsuite, or Sprout Social integration as middleware
- Webhook or Zapier trigger to notify Kara when a post is scheduled

### Analytics
- LinkedIn post performance (impressions, engagement rate, link clicks)
- X post performance (impressions, likes, retweets)
- Landing page traffic by source (UTM tracking)
- Email cadence open rates and reply rates

### Content Generation
- Brand voice guide embedded as system prompt
- One-click "generate a new post" based on a topic or URL
- Auto-check against retired terms list (no Sherpa, no em-dashes, no ChiriBrain)
- Image generation using the Chiri visual template

### CRM Integration
- When a lead submits on chiri.ai, auto-create a contact in HubSpot/CRM
- Auto-send the relevant cadence based on which landing page they came from
- Track email opens and replies back to the contact record

1. **No em-dashes** in any content, ever.
2. **No "AI Sherpa"** anywhere.
3. **No "ChiriBrain"** — always say "Chiri Platform."
4. **Always open with a question** on LinkedIn.
5. **Never reference employee ranges** in public-facing content.
6. **Never say "enterprise-grade"** — we are mid-market.
7. **Always use the real Chiri logo** in visuals — never draw a fake triangle.
8. **Always use `chiri.ai`** as the URL — `chiri.io` is the test domain only.
9. **No outbound links** in landing page body copy.
10. **Single CTA** on all landing pages: "Talk to Our Team" → `mailto:kara@chiri.ai`
