METRIC DEFINITIONS

Services Revenue — Cash collected from consulting engagement fees. One-time per engagement. Recognized when the work is delivered.

✱ ARR Booked — The total annualized value of all SaaS seat contracts signed May–Dec, as if every customer stayed for a full 12 months from signing. This is the full contract value on paper — not what you’ve collected yet.

✱✱ EOY 2026 Run Rate — The ARR meter reading on December 31. Because customers sign at different points in the year, you only capture a fraction of their annual contract value before year-end. A customer who signs in May contributes 8 months; one who signs in November contributes 2 months. This is always lower than ARR Booked — that is not a problem. It is a timing effect. The gap represents ARR that continues accruing into 2027. The EOY run rate is the number investors use to assess your exit velocity.
