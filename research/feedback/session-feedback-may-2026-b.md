# Customer Feedback Session B — May 2026

## Product Overview Discussed

- AI operating system for mid-market companies
- Three core components: data integration layer, ontology system, workflow mapping
- Workflow mapping: records browser actions, auto-annotates tasks, identifies automation opportunities
- Security: hierarchical data access controls, full auditability, SOC 2 in progress

## Top Standouts (What Resonated)

1. **Governance + safety for enterprise AI** — strong reaction to rogue agent prevention, auditability, segmented access controls. Called out as a real market gap.

2. **Self-learning system** — platform builds knowledge passively over time rather than requiring heavy manual setup. "Easier to show than tell, but powerful once trusted."

3. **Low-friction adoption / "meet you where you are"** — users don't need to fully document workflows upfront. System learns from how people already work.

4. **"Speed of the spoken word"** — turning spoken brainstorming into execution quickly, without forcing people to translate everything into written process docs first. Called out as memorable positioning.

5. **Abstraction of AI complexity** — users should not need to think about prompt engineering or ontology. The value is that AI fades into the background while people just do their jobs.

6. **Mid-market wedge** — real opening in serving mid-market companies that want to move faster than enterprise and have fewer internal blockers, while still needing governance and structure.

## Tight Messaging Hierarchy

**Core message:** Chiri helps mid-market companies adopt AI safely and actually operationalize it.

**Supporting messages:**
- Governed by design
- Learns how work gets done
- Moves at the speed of the spoken word
- Hides the AI complexity
- Built for the mid-market opportunity

## Three Punchy Positioning Statements

1. Chiri helps mid-market companies move at the speed of the spoken word, turning operational knowledge into governed AI workflows.
2. Chiri is the AI operating system for companies that want automation without losing control.
3. Chiri helps teams adopt AI without the friction of documenting everything first, learning workflows as work happens.
