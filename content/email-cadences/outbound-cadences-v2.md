## Notes on Use

**Personalization is non-negotiable.** Every Touch 1 has a `[specific signal]` placeholder. Fill it. A generic Touch 1 sent without specificity reads as automated. The signal is the proof you actually paid attention.

**Subject line discipline.** Roy Schwartz's pattern: "From [Name]," "A question on [topic]," "A note on [topic]." Invitational, not promising. Never click-bait.

**Sign-offs.** Mat signs as "Mat" in follow-ups. Full byline only in Touch 1. The shorthand feels peer-to-peer.

**No customer logos.** All customer references are anonymized as role + size + vertical until written permission is in hand.

**Lower-friction CTA option.** For mid-market segments where a large engagement triggers sticker shock, swap the default calendar CTA for: "If a 30-minute call feels heavy, we also run focused 30 to 90 day engagements scoped to the one to three workflows eating your team's week. Either way works."
